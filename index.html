How to find the actual 3D shape of a catheter with a low-resolution endoscopic camera?
In this project, a machine-learning based method is used. The shape of the catheter is assumed as a Bezier Curve with 3 control points. With the image sequence, the 3D shape information can be optimized to the true value.
